About Us

A proud veteran-owned business! At Jammin Jamaican Jerk, we are passionate about bringing the vibrant flavors of Jamaica to our community. Our recipes are crafted with love and authenticity, using traditional spices and cooking techniques to create dishes that are bursting with flavor. Whether you're a fan of spicy jerk chicken, savory curry goat, or hearty oxtails, we have something for everyone to enjoy. We are committed to providing delicious meals that not only satisfy your taste buds but also support local farmers and producers. Join us on a culinary journey to Jamaica and experience the true taste of the Caribbean!

Featured Jamaican Dishes

Spicy Jerk Chicken

Our jerk chicken is marinated in a blend of traditional Jamaican spices and slow-cooked to perfection for a smoky, spicy flavor.
